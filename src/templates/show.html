Steps to make a Video Call

Let us assume Peer P1 wants to call Peer P2

(Does not support Safari Browsers,So use Mozilla Firefox Browser)

Peer P1

  • Register and login to your account.

  • Select the user from the list whom you want to call and click the call button.

  • Now click the "Click to Start Video Chat" button.

    • Peer P2

  • The selected user should login to his account and open his HomeRoom.

  • Now click the "Click to Start Video Chat" button.

    • Maximum of 3 users can join the ChatRoom.

  • Third peer should also call the selcted user and click the

    • "Click to Start Video Chat" button to join Peer P1 and P2.
